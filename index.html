Short Bio

Massimo Equi obtained his Master's Thesis in 2018 at the University of Pisa, Italy. In 2019, he started his PhD studies at the University of Helsinki, Finland. He is part of the Genome-Scale Algorithms team, in the Algorithmic Bioinformatics group. His research interests focus on algorithms and complexity theory, with recent attention to quantum computing. His publications deal with conditional lower bounds and algorithm design for algorithmic problems arisen from bioinformatics scenarios.
